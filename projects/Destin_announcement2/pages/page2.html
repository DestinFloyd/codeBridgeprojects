Taylor Alison Swift was born December 13 1989. Born in Pennsylvania and moved to Tennessee at 14 to pursue a career in music.
She was initally a country singer signed with Big Machine Record and later signed by Sont/MTV Music publishing house. She released her debut album in 2006. Big Machine Record is a satalite company of Universal Music Group.
Taylor Swift has recieved many awards including 10 Grammy Award, 23 American Music Awards(most wins by a female artist), 12 Counrty Music Association Awards, 8 Academy of Counrty Music Awards, 1 Brit Award, and 1 Creative Atrs Emmy Award. She has been listed in Rolling Stone's 2015 list of 100 Greatest Songwriters of All Time.
